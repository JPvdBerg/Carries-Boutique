How can we help?

Answers to our most common questions.

How long does delivery take?

We courier nationwide! For Retail items (in stock), delivery takes 3-5 working days. For Custom Styles, please allow 2-3 weeks for production before shipping, as each item is handmade to your measurements.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on Retail items within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unworn with tags attached.

Please note: Custom Styles are non-returnable as they are tailored specifically to your unique measurements. If there is a fit issue due to our error, please contact us immediately.

How do I measure myself correctly?

We have a handy Size Guide on every product page! Look for the "Size Guide" button next to the size selector. For custom orders, we need your Bust, Waist, Hips, and Height in centimeters.

Where are you based? Can I collect?

We are an online-only boutique based in South Africa. To keep our costs (and prices) low, we do not have a physical showroom for fittings or collections. We courier directly to your door!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept secure online payments via Credit/Debit Card and Instant EFT. We also offer Payflex if you wish to pay in 4 interest-free installments.
