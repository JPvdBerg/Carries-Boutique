Store Policies

Shipping Policy

We courier nationwide across South Africa using trusted partners (like Aramex or PostNet).

  • Standard Shipping: R100.00 flat rate.
  • Delivery Time: 3-5 working days for major centers, 5-7 days for outlying areas.
  • Processing Time: Please allow 1-2 days for us to pack your retail order. Custom orders require 2-3 weeks for production.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

  • Retail Items: Can be exchanged for a different size if unworn and tags are attached. Customer pays return shipping.
  • Custom Styles: Since these are made to your specific measurements, we cannot accept returns unless there is a manufacturing defect.
  • Defects: If you receive a damaged item, please WhatsApp us a photo immediately for a replacement.
